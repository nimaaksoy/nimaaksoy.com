# Life in Dots Chrome Web Store Listing

This folder contains copy and visual assets for publishing Life in Dots as a Chrome Web Store new-tab extension.

## Suggested Store Listing

Name:

Life in Dots

Short description:

A thoughtful new tab that helps you see today in the context of your life and choose one small intention.

Detailed description:

Life in Dots replaces your Chrome New Tab page with a calm daily ritual.

Each new tab shows today’s date, your current day number, a visual timeline made of dots, and one small intention for the day. It is designed to help you pause before opening another page and ask what would make today count.

Life in Dots is not a prediction tool. The timeline is a reference chosen by you, and all personal information stays in your browser.

Highlights:

- See today as one dot in your possible timeline
- Write one small intention for the day
- View days, years, months, and hours
- Works offline after installation
- No account required
- No database
- No personal-data upload
- Local reset controls

Privacy:

Your birthday, name, settings, and daily intentions stay in Chrome storage on your device. Life in Dots does not send this information to a server.

Permissions:

- `storage`: used only to remember your local Life in Dots profile, settings, and daily intentions between new tabs.
